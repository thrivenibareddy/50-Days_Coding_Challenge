
# 📊 Insurance Claim & Fraud Analysis Dashboard

## 🔷 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Insurance Claim & Fraud Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI. The goal is to analyze insurance claim data and uncover insights related to claim trends, fraud detection, customer behavior, and financial risk.



## 🔷 Objectives
- Analyze claim trends over time
- Identify high-risk regions and customer segments
- Detect patterns in fraudulent claims
- Understand the relationship between premium, deductible, and claim amounts
- Provide actionable insights for business decision-making



## 🔷 Dataset Description
The dataset includes the following key fields:

- Policy details: policy number, state, premium, deductible, coverage limits
- Customer details: age, occupation, education
- Incident details: date, type, severity, location
- Claim details: total claim amount, property/vehicle/injury claims
- Fraud status: fraudulent vs non-fraudulent claims



## 🔷 Dashboard Features

### 📌 KPIs
- Total Claims: **391**
- Total Claim Amount: **21M**
- Average Claim Amount: **54.79K**
- Fraud Count: **110**
- Fraud Rate: **0.28**
- Loss Ratio: **43.85**



### 📈 Visualizations

- **Total Claims by Month**
  - Identifies seasonal trends in claims

- **Claim Amount by Incident Type**
  - Highlights major contributors to claim costs

- **Geographic Analysis (Map)**
  - Shows claim distribution across states

- **Fraud, Loss Ratio by Incident Type**
  - Helps identify fraud-prone categories

- **Premium vs Claim Amount (Scatter Plot)**
  - Analyzes relationship between premium and claims



### 🎛️ Filters (Slicers)
- Age Group  
- Incident Date  
- Incident Type  
- Policy State  



## 🔷 Key Insights

- 📉 Claims dropped significantly after February, indicating possible seasonal trends  
- 🚗 Vehicle collisions contribute the highest claim amounts  
- 🕵️ Fraud is concentrated in specific incident types  
- 🌍 Certain states show higher claim frequency and severity  
- 💰 Weak correlation between premium and claim amount  



## 🔷 Business Recommendations

- Strengthen fraud detection for high-risk incident types  
- Adjust premium pricing based on risk segments  
- Focus on high-claim regions for risk mitigation  
- Improve underwriting policies for better loss control
