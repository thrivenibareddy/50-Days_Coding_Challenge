# 📊 Employee Stress & Well-Being Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into employee stress levels and overall well-being across different departments, age groups, and work patterns. It helps organizations identify stress trends and take data-driven actions to improve employee productivity and health.


## 🎯 Key Objectives

* Monitor employee stress levels
* Analyze work-life balance (Sleep vs Work Hours)
* Identify high-stress groups
* Compare stress across departments and age groups
* Track stress trends over time



## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔢 KPIs

* **Average Sleep Hours**: 5.95 hrs
* **Average Work Hours**: 8.59 hrs
* **Average Stress Level**: 2.98
* **High Stress %**: 41%
* **Sleep vs Work Ratio**: 0.69


### 📈 Visualizations

* **Average Stress by Department**

  * Compare stress levels across Finance, IT, Marketing, Operations, and HR

* **Stress Trend Over Time**

  * Monthly analysis of stress levels (Jan–Apr 2025)

* **Work Hours vs Stress Level**

  * Scatter plot showing relationship between working hours and stress

* **Stress Category Distribution**

  * Pie chart displaying High, Medium, and Low stress levels

* **Stress & Sleep by Age Group**

  * Comparison of stress and sleep patterns across:

    * Under 30
    * 30–50
    * 50+


### 🎛 Filters (Slicers)

* Gender (Male/Female)
* Department
* Age Group


## 🧠 Key Insights

* Employees working longer hours tend to have higher stress levels
* The 30–50 age group shows higher combined stress and workload
* Some departments (like Finance & IT) show slightly higher stress
* Sleep hours are lower compared to recommended levels


## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Employee demographics (Age, Gender, Department)
* Work hours
* Sleep hours
* Stress levels (categorized into High, Medium, Low)


## 📌 Conclusion

This dashboard helps organizations proactively monitor employee well-being and identify areas where intervention is needed to reduce stress and improve work-life balance.
